About Project

Problem

Nowadays, university students in Colombia don’t know the basics of first aid, which limits their ability to act quickly in cases that require immediate attention to save a life.

Objective

Design an interactive experience aimed at university students in the city of Cali (Colombia), so they can learn first aid and emergency services

User Research

Interview

We surveyed 40 students from different universities in Cali (Colombia) using interviews and surveys. Information was collected about what they knew about first aid, if they had been in situations that required it and how they reacted to it.


Overall Results



Design Principles

As a result of analyzing the interview results, we created design principles based on the insights that were gathered from problems by the first aid entities to cover the university public, as well as how the students can help to save the life of another person immediately.


Easy Learning

The user don’t need to go to another place to learn about first aid and it should be done in a short amount of time.

Social Proof

The user wants the information provided to be true and come from a known organization.

Emergency Help

The user need a way to help during an emergency if they don’t know what to do.

Real-life Situations

The user looks for real situations that stimulate his reaction speed during an emergency.

Identification

We build a modern interface that connects young university students with the first aid world and organizations around the world.

Learn

You can easily choose a first aid course. Tapping on it will display an overview of the course and your record. When starting the course you will have a theory steps of it.

Practice

After the theory, you would do a simulation of the situation. It’s based in an interactive video where you have to react fast to save a life.

News

Read the daily news related to the medicine field and organizations like International Red Cross, St. Jhon Ambulance, etc.

Emergency

Call for help during an emergency, the AI recognize speech and would give help about of what to do, and call 911 if it is necessary.

Volunteer

Apply for a volunteer experience in International Red Cross, that fits the role you’re good at. Made with few steps rather than the Red Cross form.
