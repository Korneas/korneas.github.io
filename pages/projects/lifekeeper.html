About Project

Problem

Nowadays, university students in Colombia don’t know the basics of first aid, which limits their ability to act quickly in cases that require immediate attention to save a life.

User Research

Interview

We surveyed 40 students from different universities in Cali (Colombia) using interviews and surveys. Information was collected about what they knew about first aid, if they had been in situations that required it and how they reacted to it.

Overall Results

Design Principles

As a result of analyzing the interview results, we created design principles based on the insights that were gathered from problems by the first aid entities to cover the university public, as well as how the students can help to save the life of another person immediately.

Userflow

We create different functions to do inside Lifekeeper, that describe system behaviour for each user purpose.
List of flows we’ve covered:

  • Learn about first aid techniques
  • Read news about the medicine world
  • Call for help during an emergency
  • Apply to volunteer profile in the city
