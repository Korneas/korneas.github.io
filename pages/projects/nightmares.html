Sketch

As the start of the project, I sketched some ideas to work around, and begin thinking in types of composition, and how to integrate humans and nightmares in the same scene. I want to show the symmetry and how human perceive their monsters related to a principle of the postmodern time.

Breakdown

The composition of the scene was done in Cinema 4D using Octane as the render engine and Adobe Fuse for the human models. After the render was done, I used Photoshop for color correction and other VFX.
